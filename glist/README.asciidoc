=== Step 1 - GLib Linked List

The following program creates and prints a very basic linked list using the GLib library: link:01_glistdemo.tar.gz["GList Demo"]

....
$ ./glistdemo
Name > Tom
 Age > 15
One more? (Y/N) > y
Name > Eva
 Age > 15
One more? (Y/N) > y
Name > Hank
 Age > 17
One more? (Y/N) > y
Name > Anna
 Age > 12
One more? (Y/N) > n
Length of the list 4
Anna 12
Eva 15
Hank 17
Tom 15
....
